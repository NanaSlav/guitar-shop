How to choose the guitar

Choose guitar carefully. It should be absolutely comfortable to you. Pick a sweet sounding guitar. Listen to it in a gig or in the room. Then choose a classic amp and head and get to it. Now for your learning. First, tune the guitar. Don't play on some little finger fret. Tune it every string using the sixth fret (the one you normally hit with a thumb). If you mess up on that, go play it again and make sure you get it right. You need to know how to hit each string so you can use this as the anchor string. Also, play it out loud for an hour or so.
